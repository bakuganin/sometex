Why you choose Sometex?

Quote from the customer survey

Optimal ratio: Quality - Timely Deliveries - Price. Our purchasing priorities are listed in that order.

Quality first, then timely delivery and then price.

Availability of a safety stock is another reason, which allows us to minimize the warehouse of Fortaco Estonia.

Since Fortaco Estonia does not operate in the automotive industry, we do not need to receive parts within 1 day. However, it is very useful to have a safety stock at the supplier’s premises and be able to receive parts within 1 week. This helps to have flexibility in production planning and smoothes the situation in case of failures in the stock-keeping accounting.

Fortaco Estonia is pleased with the cooperation with Sometex.
‍

Denis Tsarev - Sourcing Manager, Fortaco Estonia
